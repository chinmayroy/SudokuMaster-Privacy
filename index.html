Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2026

SudokuMaster is built with your privacy in mind. We believe in transparency — no account required, no personal data collection, just pure sudoku enjoyment.

1. Data Collection

SudokuMaster is designed as a standalone game that respects your digital sovereignty. We do not require you to create an account, and we never collect, store, or share any personal data — such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Zero personal data footprint · No tracking cookies · No mandatory logins

2. Third-Party Services

While the game itself does not collect personal data, it may integrate trusted third-party services that help us improve stability, analytics, or distribution. These services operate under their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services – handles secure app distribution, updates, and optional achievements (if any).
  • Google Analytics for Firebase – used solely for aggregated usage statistics (like crash reports, device type) to enhance app performance. No personal identifiers are linked to your gameplay.

These services may collect anonymized data in accordance with Google's privacy policies. You can learn more at Google Privacy Policy.

3. Children’s Privacy

SudokuMaster does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us — we will take necessary steps to remove that information from our servers (though we currently store none).

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect improvements in our practices or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any modifications. Continued use of SudokuMaster after changes implies acceptance of the updated policy.

5. Contact Us

Have questions, suggestions, or privacy concerns? Reach out to us through our developer profile on the Google Play Store. We’re committed to responding promptly.
